Prohibited Activity Both Phases

These violations result in immediate account termination with no refund. No warnings. No appeals on clear violations.

  • ✗
    Exceeding contract limits — Going over your account's max contract size at any time. 1 standard = 10 micros toward your limit.
  • ✗
    Trading prohibited instruments — Stocks, options, spot forex, crypto, CFDs, or any non-CME futures instrument.
  • ✗
    Holding through news — Failing to close all positions at least 2 minutes before a scheduled tier-1 economic release.
  • ✗
    Tick scalping — Entering and exiting trades in under 2 minutes. All positions must be held a minimum of 2 minutes.
  • ✗
    Latency arbitrage — Any strategy exploiting price feed delays, quote stuffing, or system latency.
  • ✗
    Account sharing — Allowing another person to trade your account or sharing your login credentials with anyone.
  • ✗
    Cross-account copying — Using a trade copier to mirror trades across your own ONYX accounts to manipulate or inflate performance.
  • ✗
    Exploiting system errors — Taking advantage of platform bugs, pricing errors, or technical issues to generate artificial profits.
  • ✗
    All-or-nothing sizing — Placing your full maximum contract allocation into a single trade with no risk management structure.
  • ✗
    Chargebacks without contacting support — Initiating a payment dispute with your bank or PayPal without first emailing support@onyx-futures.com. This is a material breach and results in a permanent ban.
  • ✗
    Funded account inactivity — Failing to place at least one trade per calendar week on a Diamond account. Accounts suspended after 2 consecutive inactive weeks.

Glossary

Key terms explained simply.

EOD Trailing Drawdown
End-of-Day trailing drawdown. Your max loss threshold updates once per day at market close, based on your highest closing balance. Intraday dips below the threshold that recover before close do not fail the account.
Daily Loss Limit (DLL)
Max you can lose in a single session. On ONYX funded accounts this is 50% of your EOD drawdown. Hitting DLL pauses trading for the day — it does not fail the account.
Consistency Rule
A payout rule on funded accounts. No single trading day may represent more than 40% of total profits at time of payout request.
Buffer Requirement
Minimum profit threshold before your first payout. Account must be at least the full drawdown amount above starting balance (25K=$1,500 · 50K=$2,000 · 100K=$3,500 · 150K=$4,500).
Sapphire Account
ONYX's evaluation account. Trade in a simulated environment and hit your profit target while following the rules to qualify for a Diamond funded account.
Diamond Account
ONYX's funded account. Awarded after passing the Sapphire evaluation. Trade with firm capital and withdraw 80% of profits weekly.
Micro Contracts
Micro futures (MES, MNQ, etc.) are 1/10th the size of a standard contract. Your contract limit counts 10 micros as 1 standard contract.
Reset
If you breach EOD drawdown during evaluation, your account fails. Purchase a reset to restart with a fresh account at the same size. All subsequent resets are 50% off.
