FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before starting your evaluation.

A 40% consistency rule applies on funded Diamond accounts. No single trading day may represent more than 40% of your total profits when requesting a payout. This ensures consistent trading rather than one lucky day.
End-of-Day drawdown means your trailing drawdown limit only updates at market close — not intraday. If you're down during the session but recover before close, your drawdown threshold doesn't move. You get breathing room to trade without getting killed by intraday volatility.
No daily loss limit during evaluation. Your only hard limit is the EOD trailing drawdown. On funded Diamond accounts, a daily loss limit of 50% of your drawdown applies to protect the account long-term.
Up to 10 funded Diamond accounts simultaneously. Evaluations are unlimited. Each funded account is purchased and passed separately. You can copy trade across all accounts in R|Trader Pro since they all run under the same Rithmic login.
The activation fee plan lets you pay a lower monthly fee during evaluation. When you pass, you pay a one-time $99 activation fee to unlock your Diamond funded account. If you don't pass, you never pay the activation fee. Great for traders who expect to pass quickly.
Weekly. Submit a payout request from your dashboard and receive funds within 3–5 business days. No waiting a month. No complicated payout ladders.
Rithmic. You can connect with NinjaTrader, R|Trader Pro, or any Rithmic-compatible platform. Institutional-grade execution, ultra-low latency, real CME data.
$79 flat — same price regardless of account size. If you breach EOD drawdown during evaluation, purchase a reset to restart with a fresh account at the same size.
Futures contracts on CME, COMEX, NYMEX, and CBOT. This includes ES, NQ, CL, GC, ZC, ZS, and all micro equivalents. Stocks, options, forex, crypto, and CFDs are not permitted.
Flex is ONYX's freedom account. You still pass a one-step evaluation, but once funded there is zero daily loss limit and zero consistency rule — ever. A 50% consistency rule applies during the evaluation only. Your profit split also grows from 80% to 85% (payout 4) to 90% (payout 7+) and stays there permanently.
Direct is ONYX's instant funded account. No evaluation required — purchase and your Rithmic credentials are provisioned within minutes. The trade-off: tighter EOD drawdown, a daily loss limit (30% of drawdown), and a 25% consistency rule on every payout. Still offers up to 10 accounts — double the limit of every other instant funded firm in the industry.
