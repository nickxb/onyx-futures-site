FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before starting your evaluation.

A 40% consistency rule applies on all accounts. No single trading day may represent more than 40% of your total profits when requesting a payout. This applies during evaluation and on funded Diamond accounts.
End-of-Day drawdown means your trailing drawdown limit only updates at market close — not intraday. If you're down during the session but recover before close, your drawdown threshold doesn't move. You get breathing room to trade without getting killed by intraday volatility.
No daily loss limit during evaluation. Your only hard limit is the EOD trailing drawdown. On funded Diamond accounts, a daily loss limit of 50% of your drawdown applies to protect the account.
Up to 10 accounts simultaneously — evaluation or funded. Each account is purchased and passed separately. There's no free allocation — you buy and pass each account individually, just like Apex. The cap is 10 total active accounts.
Weekly. Submit a payout request from your dashboard and receive funds within 3–5 business days. No waiting a month. No complicated payout ladders from day one.
Rithmic. You can connect with NinjaTrader, R|Trader Pro, or any Rithmic-compatible platform. Institutional-grade execution, ultra-low latency, real CME data.
No time limit. Your monthly subscription keeps your evaluation active as long as you're subscribed. Take as long as you need. If you fail, purchase a reset — subsequent resets are 50% off.
Futures contracts listed on CME, COMEX, NYMEX, and CBOT. This includes ES, NQ, CL, GC, ZC, ZS, and all micro equivalents. Stocks, options, forex, crypto, and CFDs are not permitted.
